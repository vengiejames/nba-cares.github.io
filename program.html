Programs 

		

When it comes to the NBA’s social responsibility efforts, actions speak louder than words.

 The league coordinates a wide-reaching effort among the NBA family, community partners, non-profit 
organizations and business partners to benefit communities worldwide. 

 Community outreach touches every aspect of the NBA’s business, 
especially during league events like NBA All-Star and The Finals. 
he league’s community initiatives, such as NBA Cares Season of Giving, NBA Cares Week of Service and Basketball without Borders, 
present teams and players with the opportunity to give back year-round. Meanwhile, 
NBA teams maintain a range of creative programs geared to give back to their communities.

The NBA’s global reach has provided a unique opportunity to support community partner campaigns and 
initiatives that are working to combat the current issues of our time. 
NBA Cares has supported campaigns that address social issues, including the promotion of literacy, 
the eradication of malaria, and HIV/AIDS education.

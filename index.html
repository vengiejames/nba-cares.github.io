OUR MISSION

NBA Cares is the league's global social responsibility programs that builds on the NBA's mission of addressing important social issues in the United States and around the world. NBA Cares works internationally recognized youth serving programs that support education.

Through our league,players and teams,we will encourage people to donate to the cause through a variety of different ways: online donation,fund-raising events etc.
